About OBS Studio

Open-source screen recording and video editing tool designed specifically to help videographers capture and mix audios and videos to create visuals including texts and images. Teams can use filters including noise suppression and noise gate to mix audios. OBS Studio also lets managers set personalized hotkeys for actions such as muting audio sources, switching scenes, starting or stopping recordings, and more. It offers preview functionality, which lets staff view visuals before they are live. Learn more about OBS Studio
